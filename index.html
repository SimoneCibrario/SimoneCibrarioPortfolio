About me



I'm fascinated by all things concerning play in the design realm. This fascination has led me to explore various aspects of the play sphere: from my design university projects - all imbued with playful interactions, to my research work with Monobit Games, startup founded to push the cultural and technological boundaries of the videogame medium. Most recently with my MSc in games at the IT University of Copenhagen. I aim to design play and to play with design: game experiences are enriched by user centered thinking and project methodology, and our every day lives could use the ease and instinctive nature derived from play.
I seek to shape experiences capable of generating culture through play
to structure stories yet to be told

Skills

Research

During my academic journey and experience at the ludotainment startup Monobit Games, I learned how to conduct both qualitative and, thorugh data analysis, quantitative research.

UI/UX for Games
